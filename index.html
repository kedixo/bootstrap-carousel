FULL-BLEED HEADER

When to use: Full-bleed headers provide emphasis on the visual aspects of an article. Save full-bleed headers for articles with exceptional reporting, photography, or visuals that deserve extra attention from our readers. Naturally, any article with a full-bleed header should feature a gripping photo as the lead, often with a dropout headline on the photo or a standard headline beneath.
